Vowels

There are two sets of vowels: High vowels and low vowels. This is important because some particles harmonize.

High aɛiɔɯ
Low ə~ɐeɪou
In romanized orthography, the high ones can be represented with an acute accent, or by double vowels. (/a/ -> <á> or <aa>)
